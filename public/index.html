What Am I About?

Yes, that's me... A 20-year-old from the land of waffles and fries, figuring out life as I go. Not so sure what I'm doing, but that's okay...

  • I show up to lectures…most of the time
  • I learn more online than from textbooks
  • I try to stop daydreaming by making them reality...

Clueless is now what I am, it's how I approach.

Step by step, or fail by fail... What's the difference? Both the step and the fail will bring me closer to success.

  • I was 16 on my first successful fail
  • I believe in failure as much as I believe in me
  • I've got a bunch of failed ideas, mostly best kept hidden

Anyway I rather crash a few times then never get off the ground.

While working more than the average student, my income remains 0. And I don't really mind.

  • I don't aim for money, that's too realistic
  • Dreaming costs me more than it makes me
  • The ROI is there already, just not in a paycheck yet

Elon Musk or a homeless person... both can offer life-changing advice.

I've always been curious to the journey of other people. Advice, life story's, feedback or other contributions, I'll take it all.

  • I'm all ears for both your wins and fails
  • I appreciate all helping hands
  • I like having an "aha" moment, can you give me one?

Your wisdom will be forgotten if you do not pass it over.
